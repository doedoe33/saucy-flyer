An eclectic treat for connoisseurs of freak show films, this rare B-movie presents an uproarious case for alien intervention, as the human race accelerates toward self-destruction. Saucy Flyer prepares this war-torn planet for a newcomer. Will this heralded arrival be the Alien Messiah the world is waiting for or the harbinger of the end of days?

Saucy Flyer UFO PI weaves conspiracy theory, alien abduction and nuclear holocaust into an awe-inspiring satire of paranoia and fuses it before an in-your-face sideshow of musical comedy. Funny and irreverent, with a cast of zealots, patriots, aliens, gun-toters, hermits, Russkies, mutilated cattle, barflies, big guns, and an even bigger missile, Saucy Flyer UFO PI oozes the sci-fi swagger of "Plan 9 From Outer Space," the satire of "Dr. Strangelove," and a blood bath worthy of "Carrie."

Warning: this cult film is political science fiction the U.S. government doesn’t want you to see!!! A must see for abductees, conspiracy theorists and sci-fi enthusiasts alike! As the Illuminati operate in plain sight—right down to the all-seeing eye in the pyramid on the dollar bill, and the Dark Lords of the New World Order enslave the human race with reverse-engineered extraterrestrial technology. Will Saucy save the frail, carbon-based race we call humanity? Or won’t she?

A patriot whose time has come, Saucy Flyer will rock this big blue marble we call home.
