Show-up for the final shoot-out.

End times are upon us…
The Dark Lords of the New World Order enslave the human race with reverse-engineered alien technology.
The Illuminati operate in plain sight, right down to the all-seeing eye in the pyramid on the dollar bill.
Saucy Flyer is preparing this war-torn planet for an arrival of epic proportions.
Will this heralded arrival be the messiah the world is waiting for or the harbinger of the end of days?

With a cast of zealots, patriots, aliens, gun-toters, hermits, Russkies, mutilated cattle, barflies, big guns, and an even bigger missile.
Saucy Flyer flaunts the satiric quality of ‘Dr. Strangelove’, the sci-fi swagger of ‘Plan 9 from Outer Space’, and the rank stench of the Patriot Act.

“Roger Corman meets John Waters.”
